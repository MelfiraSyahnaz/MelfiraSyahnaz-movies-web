Grave of the Fireflies

In the latter part of World War II, a boy and his sister, orphaned
when their mother is killed in the firebombing of Tokyo, are left to survive on their own
in what remains of civilian life in Japan. The plot follows this boy and his sister as they do their best to survive in
the Japanese countryside, battling hunger, prejudice, and pride in their own quiet, personal battle.
